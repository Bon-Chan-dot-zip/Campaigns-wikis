Frequently Asked Questions
What exactly does Wishmakers Corporation do?
General
The Wishmakers Corporation is technically anomalous phenomena management firm. We identify, classify, contain, and resolve events and entities that fall outside conventional understanding. Our clients can range from the grandma next door to the United States government. In addition to our unique services we also produce goods for the general populace, made with techniques and materials pioneered by our research division.
Is the work dangerous?
General
That depends on your division and your personal definition of "dangerous." Field Operations agents face different conditions than, say, Client Relations staff. What we can tell you is that Wishmakers has an excellent containment record, a comprehensive insurance package, and a robust next-of-kin notification procedure that has been consistently praised for its clarity and compassion. We take safety very seriously.
What is the Corruption Index and why is mine already showing a reading?
Technical
The Corruption Index is a cumulative measure of anomalous contact over the course of your employment. An initial reading at onboarding is completely normal and indicates that your baseline tolerances have been established. A reading above 10 at onboarding is less routine but not unprecedented, and your supervisor has already been informed. The Wellness Office may reach out.
I saw something in the building that wasn't in any of the briefing materials. Who do I report this to?
Concern
Report it to your Division supervisor immediately using Form WMC/IR/INTERNAL or the form located at the bottom of this page. Do not describe it to colleagues before filing. Do not photograph it. Do not attempt to interact with it. If it interacted with you first, which, if you are asking this question, you should stop reading and fill the form immediately. Please be specific. The Emergency Response Division values specificity.
When will I receive my full clearance documentation?
Admin
Clearance documentation is issued on a continuous basis as trust is established and evaluations are completed. Most staff achieve Level 3 clearance within their first contract period. Advancement beyond that depends on factors including performance, psychological stability, and whether certain things have laid claim on you. It is worth noting that higher clearance levels permits access to our extended archives, and some personnel actively prefer not to advance. Both choices are respected here.

Lore Accordion

The First Contact Event is the official designation for the period during which anomalous phenomena first became identifiable at a global scale. Originally, the wider world, and even those who worked in related fields, did not believe that the anomalous existed. It had merely existed within fiction. During the subsequent years after the event the historical record was re-examined and several researchers noted that the anomalous had likely existed for most of human history, but accounts of it were dismissed as folk tales, mythologies, fiction, mental illness, and other such rational explanations.

Within a period of thirteen months, anomaly sightings across all 6 continents increased by an order of magnitude. Governments and organizations could not handle all the incidents through existing law enforcement and military frameworks due to the abnormal nature of the event. Wishmakers Corporation, which was then a considerably smaller consultancy operating under a different name was among the first private entities to develop a functional classification system.

The Wishmakers Corporation is cited as being the primary reason that human society has remained largely unchanged despite this event. Wishmakers has consistently been ahead of the curve when it comes to the anomalous. When the event occured, Wishmakers had rapidly adapted, and despite their low size, had greatly helped in handling anomalies globally.

The exact cause behind the First Contact is unknown. Wishmakers Corporation has put the research behind it as a tertiary priority.

Since the emergence of the anomalous on a global scale, Wishmakers has taken great carein maintaining public morale by using codenames for anomalous phenomena. Research has shown that associating the anomlous with dream or immaterial phenomena aids in preventing negative emotions when interacting with these concepts, thus one of the most common terms that is quintessential to the trade is "daydream". Now effectively synonymous with "anomaly". A daydream may refer to anything anomalous, be it an object, event, location, or entity. Daydream status is given to anything that exhibits even a smidge of anomalous traits. There is no half, one-third, or even one two-hundredths daydream. If it is anomalous it is a daydream.

Nightmare, a classification given to daydreams that pose an active threat to human, or otherwise intelligent life. Nightmares, although commonly thought of as such, are not solely given to dangerous entities, in fact, any daydream may be classified as a nightmare should it meet the criteria. This includes wonderlands, or otherwise places, events, and objects.

Wonderlands are a specific category of daydreams, like reveries they refer to a specific manner that daydreams manifest in. Wonderlands are best described as places. They often manifest with defined areas of effect. Domains, structures, and extra-dimensional spaces can all be categorized as a wonderland. Although extremely uncommon, a reverie may fully, or partially, be categorized as a wonderland, depending onits exact manner of function.

For information on Reveries, see WMC/FIELD/MNL-021.

Corruption is the process by which a subject is altered, mutated, or overwritten through exposure to, or contact with, an anomaly. This phenomenon is not limited to organic life but applies to all matter, energy, and even abstract concepts such as cognition or spirituality.

The specific effects and rate of corruption are linked to the originating anomaly; the changes inflicted upon a subject are a direct expression of that anomaly's fundamental nature. While organic beings may experience physical mutations, inorganic materials are equally susceptible, undergoing transmutation into substances or structures characteristic of the source. In rare instances, one anomaly can corrupt another. Due to the innate resistance against anomalous force, this typically requires the corrupting anomaly's influence to be overwhelmingly potent. The result may be a merging of anomalous traits, if compatible, or a complete overwriting of the weaker anomaly's properties.

The progression of corruption ceases upon the loss of contact with the anomalous source. However, its effects are permanent. Although reversing it is possible, the process is extremely difficult.

Due to the conceptual nature of anomalies, corruption extends beyond the physical to affect spiritual, cognitive, and other non-material aspects of an entity. To manage this, organizations like Wishmakers utilize a Corruption Index to quantify an individual's total exposure. This index tracks corruption on a per-anomaly basis, allowing for the correlation of specific effects with their source, which is important for determining appropriate treatment and assignment eligibility. A baseline reading of 0-5 is considered normal. Levels over 15 are generally cause for concern. While individual tolerance varies, the average non-lethal maximum is 90-100; at this threshold, the cumulative physiological and psychological effects typically result in a comatose or an otherwise indisposed of state. The accumulation of 20-25 levels from a single anomaly marks the threshold where permanent mutations begin to manifest, with severity increasing alongside the corruption level.

Reveries are, like wonderland, a specific type of daydream. Reveries are, in essence, a piece of a specific daydream, though with its core anomlous components distilled to a lesser degree. To elaborate further,there are generally two types of reveries; artificial, and natural. Here at Wishmakers Corporation our vaults contain primarily artificial reveries. They are created by analyzing contained daydreams and duplicating their anomalous properties into suitable vessels. Due to our limited technology the resulting objects contain only a fraction of their originals capabilities. Some other companies also produce their own reveries, although to a quality far lesser than Wishmakers in most aspects.

Natural reveries are functionally the same as artificial reveries. The most common method of a natural reverie manifesting is when a human experiences extreme emotional intensity. The mechanics behind this type of event has not yet been discovered. Attempts to induce such events have proven wholly unsuccessful. All future testing has been banned in accordance with the Ethics Board. Reveries created by humans have varying properties. Other methods by which natural reveries are created exist, such as in cases they are directly created by a daydream, spontaneous manifestation of a reverie without an associated entity or daydream is a noted event, possible ramifications of such events are being actively monitored.

Nearly all field operators are required to be equipped with a reverie while on duty. Evidently the vast majority of them are issued by Wishmakers rather than being personnal items, or ones issued by other companies.

Affinity is a specific and milder form of corruption that occurs exclusively between a person and a reverie. It is characterized by a progressive bond formed through repeated usage or sustained contact with a particular reverie.

At low levels, affinity manifests no noticeable effects; the reverie functions normally, and the user experiences no alterations. As the bond strengthens, higher levels of Affinity induce a temporary anomalous effect on the user whenever they are in contact with, or have the reverie equipped. This effect is directly related to the nature of the reverie itself. Concurrently, increased affinity enhances and expands the reverie's own capabilities, up to a predetermined limit correlated with its rank. Higher-ranked Reveries typically require greater affinity to unlock their full potential, while lower-ranked ones may have minimal requirements or offer no affinity bonuses whatsoever.

Unlike standard corruption, affinity is not broken by the loss of contact. Consequently, this form of corruption is permanent and cannot be cleared. However, any anomalous effects it induces on the user are entirely dependent on proximity; they vanish immediately when contact with the reverie is lost and reappear upon re-establishing it. For reveries that occur naturally, a baseline level of affinity is often already present due to the circumstances of their manifestation.
