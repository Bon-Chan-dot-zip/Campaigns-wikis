BYTHOS: I. THE FIELD

The Far Shore

A golden field of reeds glistens under the morning light.

Unseen suns cast radiant sunbeams through the scarce clouds.

Your feet still in the shallow water, yet to touch the sandbank.

Friends, family, loved ones. You see them stand amidst the ebbing stalks.

They look happy.

You know this place.

But why...
do you feel so empty?
