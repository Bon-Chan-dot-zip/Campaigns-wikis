An inanimate, inorganic, non-humanoid object in the form of a simple black and yellow bakelite test dummy.

Only anomalous property is its ability to undo damage done to it when not observed through a pseudo-temporal form of regeneration.

Although assumed to be inanimate some agents believe it may harbor a consciousness or basic cognition, sentient enough apparently to intentionally delay its regeneration if it is antagonized. (Supposedly caused by being "too rough" with it)
